Mapping and patching

A common application is mapping and patching of fluid dynamics variables, such as wall shear stress (WSS) or oscillatory shear index (OSI), obtained on the surface mesh typically by means of a CFD simulation. By construction of a harmonic function over each vascular segment, vmtkbranchmapping maps and stretches the longitudinal metric to correctly account for the presence of insertion regions at bifurcations; the additional StretchedMapping array is added to the surface.

Take a look into the Mapping and patching tutorial to learn how to map the surface of a population of vessels onto the same parametric space and enable statistical analyses of surface-based quantities
